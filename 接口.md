### 提问  
1.url: index.php/api/addQuestion    
2.post: title content tag pic   
3.response:    
{status:410, '标题长度大于20了'}   
{status:411, '问题描述长度大于100了'}    
{status:412, '请选择标签'}   
{status:200, 'ok'}  
{status:401, '只允许萌新提问哦'}    

### 回答
1.url：index.php/api/reply   
2.post: pid content pic //pid表示回复的问题的id    
3.response:    
{status:401, '只允许志愿者和萌新提问哦'}    
{status:200, 'ok'}
